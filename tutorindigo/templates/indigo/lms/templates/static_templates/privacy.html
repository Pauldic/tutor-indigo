Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 8/11/2025

The National Association of Companion Animal Rescues (NACAR) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide when visiting our website or participating in our educational programs.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information
When you interact with NACAR services, such as registering for a course, making a donation, subscribing to newsletters, or contacting us, we may collect personal information including:
  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mailing address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party processors)
  • Professional or organizational affiliation (if applicable)
b. Non-Personal Information
We may collect non-personal data automatically via cookies and analytics tools. This may include:
  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Referring website

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected to:
  • Provide access to educational courses and certificates
  • Respond to inquiries or customer service requests
  • Process donations or payments securely
  • Send newsletters, course updates, or important service announcements
  • Improve the content, design, and functionality of our website
  • Analyze user behavior to enhance user experience and site performance

3. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data only with:
  • Trusted service providers (e.g., learning management systems, payment processors) solely to support our operations
  • Legal authorities when required to comply with laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • Partner organizations only with your explicit consent, typically in cases involving co-sponsored educational events or certifications

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies and similar technologies to:
  • Maintain user sessions
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track usage for analytical purposes
You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect certain functionalities of our site, including access to your course materials.

5. Data Security
We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
However, no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage users to protect their login credentials and report any suspicious activity.

6. Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including educational partners and external resources. NACAR is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

8. Accessing and Updating Your Information
You have the right to:
  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your account or data (subject to legal obligations)
